Key Takeaways

Knowing that changes to the climate, specifically greenhouse gas levels, have such a large impact on the survival of the majority of species on Earth, including ours, it is ever so important to mitigate our impact on the environment. If even a possibility of our entire planet covered in ice exists, it should serve as a warning as to the dire consequences our planet could face in the future as we continuously warm up the Earth.
